Overview

The RFP process at VCU ensures a fair and competitive environment for acquiring goods and services. This site simplifies the process, helping departments make informed and compliant procurement decisions.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Draft the RFP using VCU's template
  2. Issue the RFP via Smartsheet
  3. Respond to questions and issue addenda
  4. Receive and evaluate proposals
  5. Conduct optional oral presentations
  6. Enter negotiations
  7. Award the agreement

FAQs & Tips

  • Q: Can I email the RFP to vendors?
    A: No. Use Smartsheet exclusively.
  • Q: What’s the biggest mistake in evaluations?
    A: Missing deadlines or scoring inconsistently.
  • Tip: Don’t score a zero unless a required response is completely missing.

Contact & Support

Reach out to Procurement Services at VCU for assistance. Evaluation committee members should follow the RFP Evaluator Guide and adhere strictly to the Conflict of Interest policies to ensure a transparent process.
