Overview

This site simplifies the VCU RFP process, ensuring departments understand each step and stay compliant while achieving efficient procurement outcomes.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Plan: Determine needs, draft scope, and timeline
  2. Draft: Use the official template and include evaluation criteria
  3. Issue: Submit and publish via Smartsheet
  4. Respond: Manage vendor questions and issue addenda if needed
  5. Evaluate: Use your evaluation team and follow COI guidance
  6. Negotiate: Shortlist top candidates and begin negotiations
  7. Award: Finalize contract and publish results

FAQs & Tips

Can I email the RFP to vendors?
No, Smartsheet is the only accepted method of submission and distribution.
What’s a common mistake in scoring?
Scoring without reading the full proposal or giving a 0 score when information is unclear but not absent.
How do I avoid a conflict of interest?
Review and sign the COI certification, and disclose any potential affiliations with Offerors immediately.

Contact & Support

Need help? Contact Procurement Services at VCU. If you’re on an Evaluation Committee, follow the Evaluator Guide and comply with all COI requirements to ensure transparency.
